## Stories

Sticky tooltip is shown on click on the element provided in `clickable` slot.
Content of the tooltip is taken from `content` slot. Tooltip is removed on click
outisde of it or when close button is clicked.

<Preview>
    <Story name="Default">
        <div style={{ width: '200px', height: '100px' }}>
            <magic-ui-sticky-tooltip>
                <p slot="clickable">Click here to open the tooltip</p>
                <p slot="content">
                    Click outisde of the tooltip or use close button to hide the
                    tooltip
                </p>
            </magic-ui-sticky-tooltip>
        </div>
    </Story>
